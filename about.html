Hello world .

I'm Meagan Moore, a front-end web developer from Toronto. I've got a passion for making fun, accessible and streamlined sites.

As a former baker, I'm no stranger to fast-paced environments and keeping an organized prep list for the day. I loved developing new menus that focused on what was in demand. Baking regularly required making something that was greater than the sum of its parts and that is exactly why I love code so much: developing is so much more than commands in a terminal. Building and debugging sites allows me to be just as creative, resilient and focused as before.

When I'm not coding, I can be found nose-deep in a new book , playing Dungeons & Dragons with my friends, writing the next great novel in my notes app or playing roller derby with the Smoke City Bandits, Toronto's longest-running roller derby team.
